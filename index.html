Scan the World is an ambitious community-built initiative whose mission is to share objects of cultural significance using democratised 3D scanning technologies, producing an extensive ecosystem of content suitable for 3D printing. In making culture accessible, communities are encouraged to share their scans, stories, and creations with the goal to bring global heritage to the masses.
